Spaghetti Pomodoro

Fantastic first dish!

Here's an easy favorite that your guests will love. It only has six ingredients and will make you look like a fine Italian chef.

A stunning but simple dish made with the freshest ingredients:

  • Pomi Organic Tomato Pulp
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Fresh Basil
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Banza Spaghetti

Preparation

  1. Heat a pan and brown a garlic clove in some extra virgin olive oil.
  2. Add the tomato, salt and a fresh basil leaf. Cook for 10 minutes.
  3. Bring a large pot of salted water to a rolling boil
  4. Add Banza, stir and reduce to a simmer.
  5. Follow package directions, cook to desired firmness, stirring frequently (expect foam).
  6. Strain and rinse with water.
  7. Add the pasta to the sauce pan after 3/4 of the cooking time along with a ladle of water and finish cooking.
  8. Stir in some extra virgin olive oil and fresh basil leaves.
